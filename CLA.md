## How to Sign

**Everyone signs, with no exceptions.** This includes Q-Summit e.V. members,
maintainers, and the core IT team: no human contributor is exempt. Only
automated bot accounts are excluded, because they cannot enter into agreements.

You do **not** need to print or email anything. When you open your first pull
request, the CLA Assistant bot will comment asking you to sign. To sign, reply
on the pull request with:

```
I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA
```

This is a **one-time** step. Once signed, all of your future contributions are
covered automatically. Your signature is recorded in this repository for our
records.
