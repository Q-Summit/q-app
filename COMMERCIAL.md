# Commercial licensing & partnerships

The Q-Summit Conference App is built by students for the events community. We
want other conferences to run good software, and we want the people who built
it to get credit for their work.

## Free for non-profits

If you **operate** a deployment as a **non-profit** (student conference, university, charity, e.V., public institution, or equivalent), you may use, modify, and
self-host the app **at no cost** under the [license](./LICENSE.md). Typical free operators include:

- student-led and university conferences,
- registered charities, non-profit associations (e.V., gGmbH, gUG, and
  equivalents), and foundations,
- public research, educational, and government institutions,

**regardless of how the event is funded.** Ticket sales, sponsorship, and grants do
not affect eligibility. What matters is whether the *operator* (the party that runs
the deployment) is non-profit. See Additional Terms §3 in [`LICENSE.md`](./LICENSE.md).

The one condition: keep the **"Powered by Q-Summit"** acknowledgment visible in
your app (see [`NOTICE`](./NOTICE)). Attribution is how the students who built
this get credit for their work.

## Commercial use

If you organize a **for-profit or commercial conference**, or want to build the
app into a paid product or service, the free license does not cover you. You
need a commercial license.

There is no fixed price list; we tailor the arrangement to the event. Options
include sponsorship, revenue sharing, co-development, or a plain commercial
license, and terms for smaller commercial events are usually modest. License
revenue keeps this student-led project running, and a license comes with
support from the people who build the app.

Contact [app@q-summit.com](mailto:app@q-summit.com) with a short
description of your event.

### Running an event for someone else?

If a **for-profit agency or company operates the app on behalf of a
non-profit's event**, the *operator* needs a commercial license. Eligibility
for the free license depends on the party running the deployment rather than
on the beneficiary. If you are unsure how this applies to you, email
[app@q-summit.com](mailto:app@q-summit.com).

## Unsure which category applies?

Email [app@q-summit.com](mailto:app@q-summit.com) and describe
your situation; we will tell you which license applies.
